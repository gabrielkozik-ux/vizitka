SMART TALK with Gabriela Mrkvicová: Guest Jan Vytřísal of IUSTORIA (Podcast)

Released: October 16, 2024 | Podcast SMART TALK

My partner Jan Vytřísal was a guest on the SMART TALK podcast hosted by Gabriela Mrkvicová (Smart Network). Jan has experience moderating social events and even working in radio — but what does he do when he's not on stage?

In the interview he shares the path that led him to founding a law firm in autumn 2022, how the name IUSTORIA came about, and why we build on transparency and honesty: the goal is a satisfied client who understands what they are paying for. We specialise in commercial law — from company formation and business model setup to disputes over unpaid invoices. And Jan saved one nice piece of personal news for the podcast itself.
