Collaboration in Business Achieves More Than Solo Effort (Jiří Urban's Podcast #07)

Released: March 7, 2025 | Podcast Jiřího Urbana #07

Jan Vytřísal and I were guests on the seventh episode of Jiří Urban's podcast. We talked about criminal law and the ethics of legal practice, but also about more personal topics — above all, how we keep a healthy work-life balance as owners of a law firm.

And about the idea that gave the episode its name: collaboration in business achieves more than solo effort. That is, after all, exactly what IUSTORIA and the communities I help build stand on. Thanks to Jiří for the invitation and a great conversation — by the way, you know his photos from my Pod Pálavou networking reports.
