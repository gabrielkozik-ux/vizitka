Reportáž: BforB setkání v Tišnově

Publikováno: 5. 2. 2026 | Autor: Mgr. Gabriel Kožík

Your most expensive investment in business is not software or marketing. It's the mistakes you didn't have to make. 🚩

Every day you carry the weight of decisions that can move your company one step forward or two steps back. You often feel alone because your employees or surroundings don't understand the pressure you are under.

Most entrepreneurs try to "sit it out" alone. We at Business for Breakfast Tišnov believe that the path to success leads through community. Our club is not about giving theoretical advice. It's about sharing concrete paths. When we share experiences, we don't just buy time – we buy peace of mind and the certainty that our next step is backed by the real practice of someone who has been there before us.

Our recent meeting took place on February 5, 2026, at Merryna bar Tišnov, and one of the speakers was Petr Grünwald (posecemevam.to, bramat.cz). He shared his personal entrepreneurial journey from the very beginnings to the present day.

Another guest was Stanislav Dejl, who works with water through the Hydrolab project and with relationships through the Dejlove project.

Our subsequent meetings are held regularly. If you want to join us sometime, do not hesitate to send a message and we will send you an invitation.
