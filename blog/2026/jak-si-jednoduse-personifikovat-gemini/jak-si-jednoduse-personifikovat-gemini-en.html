How to Easily Personalize Gemini?

Published: May 22, 2026 | Author: Mgr. Gabriel Kožík

Using artificial intelligence is no longer about writing generic queries and getting generic answers. For collaboration with an AI assistant to be truly effective, the assistant needs to know you – understanding your language preferences, background, profession, and preferred formatting style. The best way to personalize and customize Gemini to your needs is by configuring the Personal Intelligence settings, linking it with your favorite Google applications, and providing direct instructions to the assistant.

Whether you use the free version of Gemini or subscribe to advanced plans, Google offers multiple layers of customization. In this article, we'll look at how to make Gemini your ultimate personalized assistant.

🧠 Memory and Preferences (Personal Context)

The easiest way to give Gemini personality and context for your interactions is to use the built-in memory and personal instructions. You can find these settings under the Personal context (Personal intelligence) tab.

  • Activating Memory: When you enable the Memory toggle, Gemini will continuously learn from your conversations. It remembers facts you mention (e.g., your interests, projects you work on, or work style) and automatically applies them in future chats.
  • Setting Preferences (Instructions for Gemini): If you don't want to wait for Gemini to learn over time, you can give it permanent instructions directly. Simply enter your preferences in the text box. For example: "Remember that I am a software engineer, I prefer concise bullet-point answers, I communicate informally, and I am interested in cycling."
  • Managing Memory: You have full control over everything Gemini remembers about you. Under the Personal Context settings, you can view, manually edit, or delete specific items of stored information at any time.
⚠️ Important Notice for European Users: The automatic memory and chat import features from other AIs are currently (as of May 2026) subject to regional restrictions. Google has not yet officially rolled them out in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland. However, if you see the settings and wish to use these features right away, the easiest workaround is to activate a reliable VPN (e.g., set to a US location) before opening Gemini.

📥 Importing Memory and History from Other AIs

Switching to Gemini from ChatGPT or Claude but don't want to lose your hard-earned context and conversation history? Google has introduced a handy import feature, which you can find in the left sidebar menu under Import memory to Gemini.

The import works in two ways:

  1. Importing Preferences (Memory): Gemini generates a specific prompt for you. Copy and paste it into ChatGPT or Claude, which will output a summary of what it knows about you (preferences and style). Then, paste that summary back into Gemini's import window.
  2. Importing Full Chat History: Export your data from your current AI provider (typically under privacy or data controls) in a .zip archive. Upload this file directly to Gemini (supports files up to 5 GB). Gemini will process your history, allowing it to reference past conversations and continue them smoothly.

🔗 Connecting to the Google Ecosystem (Extensions)

Gemini gets a much better overview of your tasks and life if you allow it to access your data via Extensions. This connects the assistant to the Google Workspace applications you use daily:

  • Gmail and Google Drive: Allows Gemini to search and analyze your emails and documents. You can ask: "Summarize the key points from my emails about the new project this week" or "Find the budget spreadsheet for 2026 in my Drive."
  • Maps and YouTube: The assistant can plan custom routes based on your location, check transit connections, or recommend videos based on your tastes and history.

🗣️ Setting Tone and Style Dynamically in Chats

Aside from global preferences, you can adjust Gemini's behavior on the fly within individual chats:

  • Role-Playing (Personas): Define the role you want the assistant to play in your initial prompt. For example: "From now on, act as my strict fitness trainer. Speak informally, motivatially, and give me short, actionable tasks."
  • Voice and Style for Gemini Live: In the mobile app settings, you can choose from several realistic voices for Gemini Live to find the one that feels most natural for verbal conversations.

🤖 Gem Robots (Gems) – The Ultimate Level of Personalization

If you want specialized assistants for specific tasks constantly at your disposal without having to repeat instructions, the best solution is creating Gems (Roboti Gem). This feature is available for subscribers of Gemini Advanced (now Google AI Ultra) or Google Workspace.

You can create a custom Gem in less than a minute:

  1. Click on Gems in the left menu and select New Gem.
  2. Name it (e.g., "Czech Editor", "Coding Buddy", or "Interview Simulator").
  3. Write detailed instructions describing how the Gem should behave and the tone it should use.
  4. Upload files (Knowledge files) – such as a style guide, rule book, or custom documents that the Gem can reference to answer queries accurately.

If you don't want to build one from scratch, Google provides pre-made Gems, such as the Copy creator for writing help, the Hiring consultant for recruitment tips, or the Learning coach to help explain complex topics.
