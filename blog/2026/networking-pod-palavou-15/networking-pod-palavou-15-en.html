Networking Pod Pálavou #15: When ESG Meets Empathy

Published: July 22, 2026 | Author: Mgr. Gabriel Kožík

Yesterday's business meeting at the Pod Pálavou club was intense and powerful — for me personally too.

I'm genuinely glad that the benefits of social enterprise — the topic Petr Beneš from Hotel Care spoke about, a partner for integrating employees with disabilities — and the HSP mindset (highly sensitive people), covered by Michal Malaník, are topics that genuinely resonate with entrepreneurs and companies.

Petr reminded us that sooner or later, all of us will have to start taking ESG seriously — whether because of the size of our own business, or simply because we'll be supplying products and services to someone who has to. Petr, by the way, currently employs 135 people with disabilities. If you're looking for part-time help for your company, I'd recommend reaching out to him.

Michal then opened the topic of HSP with a well-known piece of folk wisdom: "Everyone is a genius. But if you judge a fish by its ability to climb a tree, it will live its whole life believing it is stupid." He debunked some myths and did a great job explaining how to work well in teams with highly sensitive people.
