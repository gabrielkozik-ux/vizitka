Reportáž: Networking Pod Pálavou #3: Podnikání napříč generacemi

Publikováno: 3. 2. 2026 | Autor: Mgr. Gabriel Kožík

Who said networking is only for „white collars“ in suits? At the Pod Pálavou community, we believe that business grows exactly where diverse expertise meets humanity. Our last meeting was a beautiful demonstration of how seemingly different worlds can complement each other. Representatives of services, as well as experts in air conditioning, windows, and electrical installations, all met at one table. It turns out that the dogma about craftsmen being too busy to „grab a coffee“ simply no longer applies.

This blending of perspectives smoothly transitioned to the next major topic of the meeting – connecting different generations within work teams. In her lecture, Šárka Pokorná from the „Můžeš podnikat“ project aptly described the different internal mechanisms that clash in today's companies:

  • Generation X and certainty: For many of us, it was crucial to have firm ground beneath our feet. A completed task was simply a matter of honor, no questions asked.
  • Generation Z and meaning: Young colleagues, however, are no longer just looking for a paycheck, but for deeper meaning. If their traditional question of „Why?“ does not receive a solid justification, their energy evaporates before they even begin.

Although it sometimes feels like a game of musical chairs played by different rules, the goal is not to fight the younger generation, but to find the key to them. Once Generation Z understands the meaning of their work, they can pour an incredible amount of energy and innovative thinking into it.

Today's fast-changing times demand a slightly different approach overall. The market fluctuates, technology overwhelms us, and many entrepreneurs feel as if they are living on an active volcano, vainly trying to control everything. Perhaps we can take inspiration from the Icelanders and their popular saying „Þetta reddast“ (meaning „It will all work out“). It's not about passivity, but a mindset that transforms crisis management into an everyday routine, and omnipresent fear into fuel for action.

This entire networking morning at Pod Pálavou confirmed once again that our community connects people looking for these deeper, meaningful, and long-term relationships without shortcuts. Thank you to all guests and members for your willingness to share your business (and life) know-how. You are the heart that beats in this club.
