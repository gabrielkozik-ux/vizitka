Reportáž: Networking Pod Pálavou #3: Podnikání napříč generacemi

Publikováno: 3. 2. 2026 | Autor: Mgr. Gabriel Kožík

This moment from Šárka Pokorná's lecture from „Můžeš podnikat“ at our last networking stirred a wave of smiles but also deep reflection in the room. We shared a fascinating insight into how the internal motor of the generations meeting in our companies today is changing:

  • Generation X and certainty: For many of us, it was crucial to have firm ground under our feet, and a completed task was a matter of honor that is not discussed.
  • Generation Z and meaning: Young colleagues are no longer just looking for a paycheck, but for meaning. If their question „Why?“ doesn't meet a solid justification, their energy evaporates before they even begin.

Maybe it sometimes feels like a game of „musical chairs“, where everyone plays by different rules. But that's exactly what it's about – learning to connect these worlds. Because when Generation Z understands the meaning, they can put incredible energy and innovation into things.

How do you have it in your teams? Do you struggle with the questions of the younger generation, or have you already found the right key to inspiration in them?

Today's networking morning at Pod Pálavou once again confirmed that our community is made of people who don't look for shortcuts but build meaningful and long-term relationships. Thank you to all guests and members for the great atmosphere and for the willingness to share business know-how. We connect entrepreneurs who want to grow and create value on the foundations of humanity and mutual trust. You are the heart that beats in the club, and we immensely appreciate everything you bring to the Pod Pálavou community.

Living on an active volcano and don't even know it? 🌋 As an entrepreneur, you face uncertainty every day. The market changes, people leave, technology overwhelms us. Most of us try to control everything. Icelanders have a different recipe for this: „Þetta reddast“. In translation, it means: „It will work out.“ But beware, it's not passivity. It's a mindset that transforms crisis management into everyday routine and fear into fuel for action.

At the next breakfast at Pod Pálavou, Anna Polehlová Jourová takes us to the north. You will learn how to apply the principle of Icelandic „managed risk“ in your company, why Icelandic children are the most creative in the world (spoiler: they are allowed to fail), and how to use limitations to finally start innovating.

Who said networking is only for „white collars“ in suits? 🛠️ In the Pod Pálavou community, we believe that business grows where expertise meets humanity. And the last meeting showed us that the most interesting synergies arise precisely between the world of services and honest craftsmanship. There is a dogma that craftsmen are too busy to go „for coffee“. The opposite is true. Experts in air conditioning, windows, and electrical installations met at one table with us. At Pod Pálavou, we connect those who want to grow and create meaningful relationships – regardless of whether you hold a pen or a tester in your hand.
