Reportáž: Networking Pod Pálavou #4: Růstové myšlení

Publikováno: 17. 2. 2026 | Autor: Mgr. Gabriel Kožík

Why are we as entrepreneurs so interested in how our children learn? Because a fixed or growth mindset starts as early as kindergarten. At Pod Pálavou, we often discuss that today's goal is not crammed knowledge, but the ability of children (and adults) to work with emotions and cooperate. As Anna Polehlová Jourová says in her lecture: „For potential to run at full throttle, we must first and foremost feel good.“

Don't let the system trample the passion for the journey just for the sake of results. Business, like upbringing, is about the courage not to be held back by history and to find meaning in what we do. When Anna Polehlová Jurová started with networking years ago, she had no idea where this path would lead her. Over time, she found that the most valuable contacts for the school often turned into deep personal friendships.

At Pod Pálavou, we believe that this journey and the sense of belonging are what truly enrich us as entrepreneurs. Because when you surround yourself with the right people, your potential runs at full throttle – in business and in life. History is a great teacher but a lousy driver – if we look at it more often than through the windshield. We often hear why something isn't possible or why we must be careful.

But look at Estonia, for example. A country that started from a much more difficult position managed to transform digitally and economically simply because it found the courage to stop holding itself back by its own past. At the Pod Pálavou club, we believe that tradition should give us roots and stability, not tie our hands when building modern, aggressive companies. Business from the heart doesn't mean doing business slowly. It means having the courage to grow with respect for the place where we live, but with an ambition that knows no borders.

Enough with excuses about market specifics. Let's rather talk about how to release those brakes together. „Trust is the hardest currency we have in business.“ These words were spoken at our meeting, and we absolutely resonates with them. At Pod Pálavou, we are not just building a database of contacts, but a safe space for sharing successes and failures. Because business from the heart means knowing that you are not alone and that you can rely on the people around you, even when a storm comes.

Thank you to all guests and members for the honesty and energy you bring to every meeting!
