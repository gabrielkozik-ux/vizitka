A Handshake or a WhatsApp Deal Can Cost You Hundreds of Thousands

Published: March 2, 2026 | Author: Mgr. Gabriel Kožík | Podcast IUSTORIA

"We shook on it" — or exchanged a few WhatsApp messages. For many entrepreneurs, that's business as usual. Legally, though, even such an informal deal can be a binding contract. The trouble starts when the parties stop agreeing on what they actually agreed.

In this episode of the IUSTORIA law firm podcast (in Czech), we discuss when an informal agreement is truly binding, how the content of verbal and chat agreements is proven in court, and why a "quick deal" can end up costing hundreds of thousands. Most importantly: the simple rules that keep you out of that situation.
