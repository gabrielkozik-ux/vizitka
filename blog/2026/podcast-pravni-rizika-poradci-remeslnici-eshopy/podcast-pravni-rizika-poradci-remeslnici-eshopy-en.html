Legal Risks for Consultants, Tradesmen and E-shops: Contracts and Complaints Done Right

Published: July 14, 2026 | Author: Mgr. Gabriel Kožík | Podcast IUSTORIA

A consultant, a tradesman and an e-shop — three completely different businesses with one shared weakness: underestimating contracts and the complaints process. Each faces different risks and needs a different setup.

In this episode of the IUSTORIA law firm podcast (in Czech), we walk through the practical risks of all three professions: liability for advice and results, defects and consumer complaints, e-shop terms and conditions. Above all — how to set up contracts and processes so they work before a dispute ever starts.

Watch the full episode above (in Czech). You'll find more episodes on the blog under the Podcasts category or on the IUSTORIA YouTube channel.
