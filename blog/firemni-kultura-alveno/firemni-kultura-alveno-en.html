Corporate Culture: An Evening with Alveno

Published: August 23, 2025 | Event Date: September 11, 2025

Pálava Horizons, together with the Alveno company, cordially invites you to an exclusive evening dedicated to a topic that drives every successful business – corporate culture. We have prepared a format full of inspiration, shared experiences, and top-level networking for you.

Who is the event for?

This event is not a standard networking meeting. We are focusing on a select group of leaders who actively manage their businesses and tackle strategic challenges. We primarily invite:

  • Business owners and executives who define the company's vision and direction.
  • C-level managers (CEO, COO, CTO) who are responsible for key areas of the company.
  • Established entrepreneurs who are intensively dealing with operational and business matters and are looking for inspiration for further growth, not just primarily acquiring clients.
The goal is to create a platform for a deeper discussion on how values, processes, and people management shape a sustainable and successful business. We want to share what really works in practice.

What can you expect?

The program is designed to connect strategic insights with practical workshops:

  • Two panel discussions with leaders from various fields.
  • Four specialized workshops on topics such as pricing, B2B relationships, leadership, and working with the younger generation.
  • A tour of the Alveno facilities, a leader in HR digitalization.
  • A concluding networking barbecue in a relaxed atmosphere.

Key Information

  • Date and Time: Thursday, September 11, 2025, 4:00 PM – 8:30 PM
  • Location: Alveno, Purkyňova 99, Brno
  • Price: 2,500 CZK
