Test Your Senses to the Max! Report from the 14th Meeting of the Pod Pálavou Club

Published: October 8, 2025

Seeing the World Differently

At Tuesday's club meeting, we experienced what it's like to rely only on hearing, touch, and taste with Irena Jelínková. With our eyes covered, we tried to memorize a text about Maria Theresa, blindly drew a bizarre monster based only on Michal Malaník's description, and in pairs, guessed what food the other was serving us. It was an incredible experience that opened our eyes, even though they were closed.

And what about cooperative musical chairs with Anna Polehlová Jourová? Forget about competition and sharp elbows. The goal was to cooperate so that we all fit on the dwindling number of chairs, and no one was left out of the game.

You can recall the great club atmosphere in the photos from Jirka Urban.
