My Mission

My mission is to transform AI compliance from a regulatory hurdle into a strategic advantage that enables companies to innovate safely and effectively.

How I Can Help You

Pillar 1: AI Compliance & Governance

I help companies implement AI safely and in compliance with European legislation (AI Act). My goal is to turn regulatory obligations into your competitive advantage.

Strategy & Implementation

  • Fundamental Rights Impact Assessment (FRIA)
  • Setting up internal processes and policies
  • Training for employees and management

Legal & Technical Certainty

  • Preparation for certification under the AI Act
  • Ensuring compliance with GDPR and other regulations
  • Monitoring and reporting obligations

Pillar 2: Legal Services for B2B

I provide specialized legal advice that protects your innovations and supports healthy growth.

Specialization

  • E-commerce: Legal certainty for your online business.
  • Intellectual Property: Protection of your brand, software, and creative works.
  • Commercial Law: Clear contracts that minimize risks.
  • Corporate Law: Company formation and corporate agenda, M&A.
  • Competition Law: Protection and enforcement of rights in a competitive environment.
  • Labor Law: Setting rules for the use of AI in the workplace.

About Me: A Union of Two Worlds

My professional journey is a logical connection of two passions: the precise world of law and the dynamic world of technology. As a lawyer specializing in commercial and technology law, I have always stood on the side of innovators. Gradually, I realized that a real competitive advantage comes not only from legal protection but from the active use of technology. That's why I delved into the field of artificial intelligence to offer my clients not just certainty, but also a catalyst for their growth.
