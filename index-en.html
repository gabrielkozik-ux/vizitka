What Business Owners Ask Me

Does AI write your contracts?
I do create contracts with AI — and I'm open about how. Using the right tools, I first map your situation, the typical pain points of your kind of business and the impact on everyone involved. I brainstorm what you'll need tomorrow, not just today. The result is a strategic concept for a contract designed to deliver beyond your expectations. But no output ever leaves without my review and a check against the law and case law — the full professional and reputational responsibility for every contract always rests with me.
Is my company data safe with you?
Yes — it's the foundation of my practice. I work exclusively in a closed, secured environment; no client data ever passes through public AI tools. And I'll teach you how to set up the same protection in your own company.
Do I need to understand technology for us to work together?
Not at all. My role is that of a translator: I turn the complicated world of law and technology into clear business language. You understand your business — I'll take care of the rest.
What if we're not dealing with AI at all yet?
That's fine — possibly even an advantage. Most problems I solve come from rushed adoption without rules. We'll start with what your company actually needs: from contracts and data protection to the first safe steps with AI, when the time comes.
